## 1. Authentication

- **Registration**: Create a new account using your name, email, and password.
- **Login**: Securely access your financial data using your credentials.
- **Session**: Your login is secured via JWT. You will remain logged in until you choose to log out.

## 2. Dashboard

The Dashboard is your financial control center.
- **Key Stats**: Quick view of your Total Balance, Monthly Income, and Monthly Expenses.
- **Filtering**: Use the period buttons (**Monthly**, **Yearly**, **All**) to filter the stats and charts.
- **Expense Distribution**: A pie chart showing exactly where your money is going.
- **Recent Activity**: A list of your most recent 5 transactions.

## 3. Transactions

Manage your money in detail here.
- **Add Transaction**: Click the "Add Transaction" button to record a new entry. Specify the type (Income/Expense), amount, date, category, and a brief description.
- **View History**: A full table of all your transactions.
- **Delete**: Remove incorrect entries using the trash icon.

## 4. Categories

Personalize your tracking.
- **Create**: Add custom categories for both Income and Expenses to better organize your ledger.
- **Clean up**: Delete categories you no longer need.

## 5. Reports

Get deep insights into your financial health.
- **Chronological Trend**: A 6-month line chart comparing your Income vs Expenses.
- **Income Summary**: See which sources are bringing in the most money.
- **Expense Summary**: Identify your highest spending areas at a glance.

## 6. Profile Management

Keep your account secure and up to date.
- **Update Name**: Change your display name.
- **Security Check**: Update your password regularly. You will need to provide your old password for verification.
- **Alert System**: Look out for the color-coded alerts at the top of the page for success or error confirmations.
